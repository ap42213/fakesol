# Privacy Policy for FakeSOL Wallet Extension

**Last Updated: December 29, 2025**

## Overview

FakeSOL Wallet ("the Extension") is a browser extension that allows users to interact with Solana Devnet applications. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Private Keys**: Stored locally in your browser's encrypted storage (chrome.storage.local). Never transmitted to any server.
- **Public Keys**: Derived from your private key, used to display your wallet address and interact with Solana Devnet.

### What We Don't Collect
- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your browsing activity
- We do not use analytics or tracking tools
- We do not transmit any data to external servers

## Data Storage

All wallet data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. Your private keys never leave your browser.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension connects to:
- **Solana Devnet RPC** (api.devnet.solana.com) - To fetch balance and submit transactions
- **Solana Explorer** (explorer.solana.com) - External links only, no data shared

## Security

- Private keys are stored in Chrome's local storage
- No data is transmitted to FakeSOL servers
- The extension only works on Solana Devnet (test network)
- No real funds or mainnet access

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all stored data by removing the extension
- Export your private key at any time
- View exactly what data is stored via Chrome DevTools

## Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13.
